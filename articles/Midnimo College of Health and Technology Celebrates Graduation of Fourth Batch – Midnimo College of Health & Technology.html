Midnimo College of Health and Technology Celebrates Graduation of Fourth Batch

  • Midnimo
  • September 26, 2024

As-salamu alaykum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh. First and foremost, all thanks and praise to Allah (SWT) for making this momentous occasion possible.

On August 4, 2020, Unity College of Health and Technology proudly celebrated the graduation of 27 students who successfully completed their courses in Nursing and Pharmacology. This group marks the fourth batch of graduates from the Unity Institute, and their achievements reflect the dedication and hard work they have invested over the past years.

The graduation ceremony was held at the Galdogob Green Park Hall and was graced by the presence of esteemed government officials, community leaders, and proud family members. Distinguished guests included:

  • Minister of Education: Abdullahi Mohamed Hassan
  • Minister of Security: Abdisamad Mohamed Galan
  • Deputy Minister of Finance
  • Deputy Minister of Health
  • Deputy Minister of Justice
  • Commander-in-Chief of Puntland Police
  • Puntland and Federal Representatives
  • Regional and District Education Coordinators

Also in attendance were parents, businessmen, youth, and fellow students, all gathered to celebrate the accomplishments of the graduates.

The presence of such high-ranking officials underscores the importance of education and healthcare in our community. Their support highlights a shared commitment to advancing educational opportunities and improving healthcare services within our region.

Unity Institute remains steadfast in its mission to provide quality education and to nurture the next generation of healthcare professionals. We are dedicated to continuing this legacy and are always ready to produce knowledgeable and skilled students, Insha’Allah.

To the graduates: Your journey does not end here. As you step into your professional lives, remember that you carry the hopes and expectations of your community. We believe in your potential to make significant contributions to the healthcare sector and to be agents of positive change.

Once again, we extend our heartfelt gratitude to everyone who attended and supported this event. May Allah bless our graduates and guide them in their future endeavors.
